In-App Support

Signed-in users can also go to Profile > Help & Support to submit a ticket with optional device details and a screenshot.

Account Deletion

To delete your account in the iPhone app, go to Profile > Privacy & Data > Delete Account. On Android, go to Profile > Delete Account. If you cannot access the app, email support from the address on your Nomfly account and include "Delete my Nomfly account" in the subject.

Subscriptions

App Store and Play subscriptions are managed by the store where you purchased them. You can cancel or manage billing from your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.
