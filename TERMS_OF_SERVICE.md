## 3. AI-Enhanced Features

Nomfly uses AI to enhance parts of the experience. We are transparent about what is and isn't AI-generated.

### What IS AI-Enhanced

- **Meal suggestions & ranking:** Decide picks and mood/meal-type recommendations are generated by large language models (Anthropic Claude)
- **Food images:** Recipe card photos are AI-generated (Google Gemini) when no source photo is available
- **Metadata tagging:** Cuisine, category, difficulty, style tags (e.g., Quick, Comfort Food), mood hints, and dietary labels on user-submitted public recipes are assigned by AI and may be imperfect
- **Nutritional values:** Calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, and sodium are *estimates* computed either by AI (Claude) based on ingredient quantities, or derived from USDA FoodData Central lookups with regex-parsed ingredient matching — not lab tests. Actual values vary with preparation, brand, and portion size and may be off by 20–40% in either direction.
- **Recipe quality screening:** User-submitted public recipes are AI-audited for spam/non-food content before publishing

### What is NOT AI-Altered

- **Ingredients lists, cooking steps, prep/cook times, and serving sizes** are stored as provided by the original recipe source (catalog import, URL extraction, or direct user submission). Nomfly does not rewrite, substitute, or generate these fields with AI.

### Limitations You Accept by Using Nomfly

- **Not medical or nutritional advice:** Nomfly does not provide medical, nutritional, or dietary counseling. Consult a qualified healthcare professional or registered dietitian for specific dietary needs, allergies, medical conditions, pregnancy, or health goals.
- **Not FDA-evaluated:** Nutritional estimates have not been evaluated by the FDA and are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
- **Not a substitute for professional guidance:** If you have severe food allergies, metabolic conditions (including diabetes), kidney/liver disease, heart conditions, or other health concerns, do not rely solely on Nomfly's suggestions or nutrition estimates.

## 3b. Allergens & Dietary Restrictions

**You are responsible for verifying every ingredient for allergens and dietary compatibility before cooking or eating.** Nomfly's dietary filters (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, keto, pescatarian) work by pattern-matching ingredient text and are best-effort only. Filters may:

- Miss hidden allergen sources (e.g., honey in "vegan" marked recipes, trace gluten in sauces)
- Miss ingredient substitutions written in non-standard ways
- Surface recipes with unlisted cross-contact ingredients

If you have a life-threatening allergy, do not rely on app filters. Read every ingredient and verify with the original recipe source when in doubt.

## 3c. Food Safety

You are responsible for safe food handling and preparation. Cooking times, temperatures, and instructions in Nomfly are guidelines only. Always:

- Cook meat, poultry, seafood, and eggs to safe internal temperatures recommended by the USDA or equivalent food safety authority in your region
- Follow safe storage, thawing, and reheating practices
- Discard recipes that appear undercooked, unsanitary, or mismatched from the stated description
- Consider your kitchen equipment, altitude, and ingredient freshness — AI-suggested times may not account for these
